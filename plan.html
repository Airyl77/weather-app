Learning Principles 🧭

⚡Build something every week — even a tiny component. Projects beat tutorials every time.
🔁Revisit earlier concepts as you add new ones. Spaced repetition is how you make things stick.
💬When stuck, describe the problem out loud (rubber duck debug) before googling. Forces clarity.
📦Commit to GitHub daily — even broken code. Your commit history IS your portfolio.
🎯Read official docs (React, MDN, Node) — they're better than most courses at this point.
